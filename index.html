What is Series List?



Series List is a website where you can make lists of whatever you want.

You can make lists of series, movies, books, games, food, resturants, recepies, etc.

You can even make lists of lists!

There are also tools for rating and commenting your lists in whatever way you like.

What can you do here?



Here you can make a cool list!

You can make lists of series, movies, books, games, food, resturants, etc.

You can even make lists of lists!

There are tools for editing your title, putting a simple categorization on your item and of course deleting your items.

To get started you can click on the "List" button in the header or the "Get Started!" button below. There is also a "Help" button in the header if you need help.
