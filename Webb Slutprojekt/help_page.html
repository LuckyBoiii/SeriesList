Help

Here you can see the list settings. This help page will go over what they do one by one.

The first thing you can change is the page title, by clicking and then typing in. The title will be saved but you can change it whenever by clicking and typing again. It is currently marked on the page as "Place your list title here!"

Next is the title of your new item. This you write in the "Write your title here!" area. Your title can be as long as you want, but will not be displayed if it goes outside the screen. Every title is editable even after adding the item to your list.

Next up is the "Series" and "Other" checkboxes. If you check a box your item will be marked as either a series or other. This is useful for quickly looking through your list and seeing if you item is a series which you can watch or something else.
