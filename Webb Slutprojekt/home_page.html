What is Series List?



Series List is a website where you can make lists of whatever you want.

You can make lists of series, movies, books, games, food, resturants, recepies, etc.

You can even make lists of lists!

There are also tools for rating and commenting your lists in whatever way you like.

Titles



Add titles to your list items! The title can be whatever you choose, it's all up to you!

Of course you can change the title whenever you wish.

Rate your items



Put your own rating on your items. Simply click the stars to change your score.

This is especially usefulll for lists of series, movies, foods and resturants!

Add comments to yor items



Comment your items for a longer description of them!

You can even use this to store recepies. You can change the added comment at any time.
